The most important symptoms of coronavirus are:
  • a high temperature
  • a new, continuous cough
  • a loss or change to your sense of smell or taste
! Warning If they have any of these most important symptoms, they should stay at home until they get their result. They can leave home to get or post a test.
Additional symptoms include:
  • a headache
  • feeling tired and exhausted
  • muscle aches
  • a loss of appetite
If they have only one or more of these additional symptoms, they do not need to stay at home while waiting for their result, unless they've been asked to.
