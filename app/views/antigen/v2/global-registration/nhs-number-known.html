Can you provide your NHS number?

This number helps us quickly:
  • save your test result to your GP record
  • make your results available if you need to prove you do not have COVID-19
It's OK if you do not have it, you can still continue. But not providing your NHS number could cause delays if you wish to attend certain events.

What it looks like

It's 10 digits long, and usually on perscriptions or a hospital or GP letter.

In Scotland, it's known as a CHI number, in Northern Ireland, it's known as an H&C number.
