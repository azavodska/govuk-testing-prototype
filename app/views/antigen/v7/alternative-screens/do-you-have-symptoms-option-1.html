The main symptoms of coronavirus are:
  • a high temperature
  • new, continuous cough
  • loss or change to your sense of smell or taste
Additional symptoms include:
  • a headache
  • feeling tired and exhausted
  • muscle aches
  • a loss of appetite
! Warning If you have any of these symptoms, you and anyone you live with should stay at home until you get your result.
