When to get the test

You need to order the home test in the first 4 days of having symptoms.

If you live in Leicester, where there is a coronavirus outbreak, you can get a test even if you do not have symptoms. Order your test now.

Who can have it

You can get a test kit for yourself and up to 3 other people you live with who have coronavirus symptoms.

In England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland, a child can have a test whatever their age.
