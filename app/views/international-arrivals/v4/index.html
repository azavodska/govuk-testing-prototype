Order and pay for coronavirus (COVID-19) tests if you're travelling to the UK from a country that's not on the red list. You need to do this at least 2 days before you arrive in the UK. Check the correct travel test package for you (see links under start button).

  • A travel test package of 1 PCR test (day 2) costs £68
  • A travel test package of 2 PCR tests (day 2 and day 8) costs £136

We'll send tests to the UK delivery address you give us. You should get them in time to take your day 2 test.

Day 1 counts as the day after your arrival in the UK.

Who is this for?

You can order travel test packages for yourself and other people. They must be travelling with you and have the same delivery address as you.

What you need before you start

  • Delivery address in the UK
  • Passport number
  • Flight or Eurostar number or ferry name
  • Payment card (international payment cards are accepted)
  • Email address
  • Mobile phone number

How to get help with your order

If you need any help with your order, including changing it, getting a refund or replacing a test kit, call:

  • 119 from the UK
  • +44 (0) 151 676 0491 from outside the UK

Lines open 7am to 11pm.
