Order and pay for coronavirus (COVID-19) tests if you're travelling to the UK from a country that's not on the red list. You need to do this at least 2 days before you arrive in the UK.

  • A travel test package of 1 PCR test (day 2) costs £68
  • A travel test package of 2 PCR tests (day 2 and day 8) costs £136

We'll send tests to the UK delivery address you give us. You should get them in time to take your day 2 test.

Day 1 counts as the day after your arrival in the UK.

Who is this for?

You can order travel test packages for yourself and other people. They must be travelling with you and have the same delivery address as you.

What you need before you start

  • Delivery address in the UK
  • Passport number
  • Flight or Eurostar number or ferry name
  • Payment card (international payment cards are accepted)
  • Email address
  • Booking reference number (if you're replacing test kits)

How to request a refund or replacement test kit

To request a refund, call this number before your test kit is sent (usually the day you're due to arrive in the UK) or we cannot guarantee a full refund. You'll need your order details, including your booking reference number.

You can also call this number to arrange a replacement kit.
