As an organisation manager, this service lets you manage site access for new and existing staff. You can:

  • add sites where your organisation can log test results and collections
  • add and manage team leaders, so they can approve or deny site access for staff
  • work as a team leader to manage site access for staff

How to create an account

You'll receive a text with your temporary password. You must use this wiithin 28 days before it expires. Sign in with your email address as your username. Then reset your password to continue.

If you're a team leader, use the same login details you already have.

Start now at: https://org-admin.log-coronavirus-test-site-results.service.gov.uk
