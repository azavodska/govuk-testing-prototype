‘PCR tests’ are mainly for people who have coronavirus symptoms.

You have told us one of the following:

  • you do not have symptoms and are not eligible for a PCR test for other reasons
  • your symptoms started too long ago

Getting tested if you have no symptoms

In England

Most people without COVID-19 symptoms do not need to get rapid lateral flow tests. Check who should get lateral flow tests (opens in a new tab).

In Scotland, Wales or Northrem Ireland

You can get a rapid lateral flow test if you do not have symptoms.

The following links open in a new tab. Find out how to:

  • get a lateral flow test in Scotland
  • get a lateral flow test in Wales
  • get a lateral flow test in Northern Ireland

Getting tested if you're travelling to another country

If your travel company has asked you to get tested, and you do not have symptoms, you can pay for a private test.
