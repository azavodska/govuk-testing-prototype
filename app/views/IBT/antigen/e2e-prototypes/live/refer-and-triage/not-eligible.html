In England

Most people without COVID-19 symptoms do not need to get tested. Check who should get rapid lateral flow tests.

In Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland

If you have rapid lateral flow test kits at home, use these first.

If you do not have any at home, then picking up kits from a pharmacy is usually the quickest way to get a rapid lateral flow test for most people.

Getting tested if you're travelling to another country

If your travel company has asked you to get tested and you do not have symptoms, you can pay for a private test.

Getting help

If your symptoms get worse or you need medical help:

  • use 111 online (opens in new tab)
  • call 111 if you're in England, Wales or Scotland
  • call a GP or GP out-of-hours service if you're in Northern Ireland
  • call 999 if you feel very unwell or think there's something seriously wrong
