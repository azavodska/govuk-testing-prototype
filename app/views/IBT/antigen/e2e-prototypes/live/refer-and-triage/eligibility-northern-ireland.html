You are eligible to get a PCR test if you have any coronavirus symptoms.

! Warning If you have a high temperature, a new continuous cough, or loss or change to your sense of smell or taste, you should self-isolate straight away. You can leave home to get or post a test.

Find guidance for self-isolation (opens in new tab).

You may also be eligible if you:

  • have been identified as part of an eligible group
  • have been asked to get a follow-up test
  • need to get a test for someone you live with who has symptoms
  • are a health and social care worker
  • are a close contact of a health and social care worker

Find out if you need to get a PCR test (opens in a new tab) .

If you're worried

If your symptoms do not get better, or get worse, contact your GP or your GP out of hours service. In an emergency dial 999.
