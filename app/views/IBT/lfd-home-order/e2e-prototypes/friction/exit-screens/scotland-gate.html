In Scotland, everyone can order free rapid lateral flow tests until 18 April 2022.

From 18 April, you can only get free rapid lateral flow tests if you're (the following links open in a new tab):

  • fully vaccinated and have been in close contact with someone who tested positive
  • eligible to end self-isolation(opens in new tab) early so need to take tests on days 6 and 7
  • eligible for COVID-19 treatments(opens in new tab)
  • visiting a care home or hospital
  • an unpaid carer(opens in new tab)

If you are not eligible for tests

If you have symptoms, you should book a PCR test online or call 119.

Find out what to do if you think you have COVID-19. If you're worried, or your symptoms get worse, speak to your GP or contact 111. In an emergency dial 999.

You can also find advice and support if you're worried about COVID-19.
