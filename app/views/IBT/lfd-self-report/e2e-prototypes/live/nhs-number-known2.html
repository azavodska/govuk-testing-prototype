Can you provide your NHS number?

This number helps us quickly save your test result to your NHS COVID pass.

In England, you may be asked to show this pass to get into events, or to travel abroad.

! Warning Not providing your NHS number could cause delays if you wish to attend certain events.

What it looks like

It's 10 digits long, and usually on prescriptions or a hospital or GP letter.

In Scotland, it's known as a CHI number. In Northern Ireland, it's known as a H&C number.
