Order and pay for coronavirus (COVID-19) tests if you're travelling to the UK from a country that's not on the red list. You need to do this at least 2 days before you arrive in the UK.

A travel test package costs £68 and includes a PCR arrival test. You can take the test after you arrive in the UK as soon as you receive it, but not later than day 2. Day 1 counts as the day after your arrival in the UK.

Who is this for?

You only need to order a travel test package if you do not qualify as fully vaccinated and you're aged 18 and over.

You do not need a travel test package if you:

  • qualify as fully vaccinated
  • are aged 17 and under (even if you're not vaccinated)
  • Check if you qualify as fully vaccinated or not in England
  • Check if you qualify as fully vaccinated or not in Scotland
  • Check if you qualify as fully vaccinated or not in Wales
  • Check if you qualify as fully vaccinated or not in Northern Ireland

You can order travel test packages for yourself and up to 9 other people. They must be travelling with you and have the same delivery address as you.

What you need before you start

  • Delivery address in the UK
  • Passport number
  • Flight or Eurostar number or ferry name
  • Payment card (international payment cards are accepted)
  • Email address
  • Unique booking reference (if you're replacing test kits)

How to request a refund or replacement test kit

To request a refund, call this number before your test kit is sent (usually the day before you're due to arrive in the UK) or we cannot guarantee a full refund. You'll need your order details, including the unique booking reference.

You can also call this number to arrange a replacement kit.

For more details see our terms and conditions.
