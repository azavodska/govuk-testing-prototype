Follow these tips to take a good photo

  • all 4 corners of the test strip are in the outline
  • place the test strip the right way up (C & T on the right-hand side)
  • check the image is not blurry
  • the test strip is well lit with minimal shadows or reflections

  • lay the test strip flat on a plain surface
  • ensure all 4 corners of the test strip device fit inside the outline
  • make sure no personal information is in the photo (e.g. paperwork with a name, date of birth, address or bank details)
