Order and pay for coronavirus (COVID-19) tests if you’re travelling to the UK from amber or green list countries. You need to do this at least 2 days before you arrive in the UK.

  • A travel package of 2 tests (day 2 and day 8) costs £210
  • A travel package of 1 test (day 2) costs £105

We'll send tests to the UK delivery address you give us. You should get them in time to take your day 2 test.

Day 1 counts as the day after your arrival.

Who is this for?

You can order COVID-19 tests for yourself and other people. They must be travelling with you and have the same delivery address as you.

If your travel plans change after ordering and paying for your tests, you can change your order or get a refund.

What you need before you start

  • Delivery address in the UK
  • Passport number
  • Flight, ferry or Eurostar number
  • Payment card (international payment cards are accepted)

If you need help with your order, call 119.
